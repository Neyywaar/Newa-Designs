Jojolappa!
Welcome to our website dedicated to the Newari community, a vibrant and culturally rich group hailing from the Kathmandu Valley of Nepal. Our website offers a unique shopping experience for those interested in purchasing traditional Newari attires and costumes, from elegant sarees to ornate jewelry and accessories. Our collection is carefully curated to showcase the intricate details and designs that are integral to Newari culture and heritage.

In addition to our online store, we also host a variety of cultural events and programs to promote and preserve Newari traditions. These include the colorful Lakhey Jatra festival, the delicious Newari Bhoj feast, the mesmerizing Newari Cultural Dance performances, and the solemn worship of the Kumari, the Living Goddess of Nepal. We take pride in our commitment to sharing these customs with others and ensuring that they are passed down through generations.

We invite you to explore our website and learn more about the fascinating Newari culture. Whether you're looking to purchase traditional attire, attend cultural events, or simply gain a deeper understanding of this unique community, we are here to serve you. Thank you for visiting!
